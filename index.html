⚠️ Current KRA Enforcements to Be Aware Of:

1. eTIMS is mandatory for all businesses — The penalty for not issuing eTIMS receipts is up to KES 1,000,000 or 3 years imprisonment. This applies to all sales of goods or services.

2. Nil Returns must still be filed — Even with no income, all KRA PIN holders must file annual returns. Late filing attracts a KES 2,000 penalty per year.

3. Rental Income must be declared — KRA is actively matching rental payments to tax records. Undeclared rental income is subject to a 20% penalty plus interest.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions from business owners and landlords.

Do I need to file Nil Returns if I had no income?
Yes. Every person with a KRA PIN must file an annual tax return, even if the income is zero. Failure to file results in a penalty of KES 2,000 per year. Our Starter Package covers this.
Is eTIMS required for small businesses?
Yes. The law requires all businesses supplying goods or services to issue electronic tax invoices through eTIMS. Non-compliance can lead to significant penalties of up to KES 1M.
Do you access my KRA account?
We guide you through the process on the official KRA portals (etims.kra.go.ke and itax.kra.go.ke). You maintain full control of your login credentials. We do not store your passwords.
Do you offer services outside Nairobi?
